H Configuration Engine

An H configuration engine is a piston engine with two banks of cylinders arranged in an H-shaped configuration. The cylinders are typically aligned in parallel and share a single crankshaft.

H configuration engines are rare and have been used in a few experimental and concept vehicles due to their unique design and packaging advantages.
