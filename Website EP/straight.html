Straight Engine

A straight engine, also known as an inline engine, is a piston engine with cylinders arranged in a single straight line along the crankshaft. This configuration is simple and space-efficient, making it popular for many applications.

Straight engines come in various configurations, including inline-4, inline-6, and inline-8, with the number denoting the number of cylinders.
